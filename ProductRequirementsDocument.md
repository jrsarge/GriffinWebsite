Product Requirements Document (PRD)Project: Griffin Rossberg Professional Portfolio1. Executive SummaryDevelopment of a personal portfolio website for Griffin Rossberg, a Mechanical Engineering student at the University of Utah. The product serves as a central hub to demonstrate CAD expertise, engineering experience, and technical skills to prospective employers.2. Market Research & Analysis2.1 Problem StatementTraditional resumes (PDFs) fail to adequately showcase 3D modeling work, complex engineering projects, and the "personality" of a candidate. LinkedIn is restrictive in formatting.Pain Point: Recruiters cannot visualize the complexity of "Winch Mount Project" or "Bridge Simulation" text descriptions.Pain Point: Static documents do not demonstrate technical versatility (e.g., coding ability).2.2 Target Audience (ICP)Primary: Engineering Hiring Managers (Mechanical/Civil/Automation).Secondary: Technical Recruiters / HR Screeners.Tertiary: Peers/Collaborators.2.3 User PersonasRecruiter Rachel:Goal: Quickly verify education (U of U) and specific skills (SolidWorks CSWP) in < 30 seconds.Behavior: Scans for keywords; downloads PDF resume.Needs: Clear hierarchy, "Download Resume" button immediately visible.Engineering Manager Mike:Goal: Assess technical depth and problem-solving ability.Behavior: Clicks into specific projects; looks at "Problem -> Solution" narratives; evaluates CAD screenshots.Needs: Detailed project descriptions, clean visual presentation, mobile responsiveness.3. Functional RequirementsNavigation: Sticky/Fixed header with smooth scrolling or tab switching.Theming: Dual-theme support (Forest/Bright) to showcase design sensibility.Content Sections:Home: Hero section with elevator pitch.About: Personal biography and photo.Projects: Card-based layout with tags (Professional vs. Academic).Resume: Digital HTML rendering of the resume + PDF Download.Contact: Functional form (or mailto link) + Social links.Tech Stack: React (Vite), Tailwind CSS, Vercel Hosting.4. Non-Functional RequirementsPerformance: Lighthouse score > 90.Accessibility: WCAG 2.1 AA compliant (color contrast, aria-labels).Responsiveness: Fully functional on Mobile (iPhone SE up to Pro Max) and Desktop.SEO: Indexed for "Griffin Rossberg Mechanical Engineer".
