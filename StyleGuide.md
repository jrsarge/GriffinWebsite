Style Guide1. Tone of VoiceProfessional: Confident but humble.Technical: Precise engineering terminology (e.g., "Tolerance," "GD&T," "FEA") is encouraged.Concise: Bullet points preferred over long paragraphs.2. Color PaletteTheme A: Forest (Professional/Engineering)Primary: Emerald 900 (#064e3b) - Headers, Navbar.Accent: Emerald 600 (#059669) - Buttons, Links.Background: Stone 50 (#fafaf9) - Warm off-white.Text: Stone 800 (#292524) - Dark grey for readability.Theme B: Bright (Modern/Tech)Primary: Blue 600 (#2563eb) - Blueprint Blue.Accent: Orange 500 (#f97316) - Highlights/CTAs (Complementary).Background: Slate 50 (#f8fafc) - Cool white.Text: Slate 900 (#0f172a).3. TypographyFont Family: System Sans-Serif (Inter, Roboto, or Segoe UI). Clean, readable, geometric.Headings: Bold, Tracking-tight.Body: Regular weight, comfortable line-height (1.6).4. ComponentsButtons: Rounded corners (Tailwind rounded-lg). Solid fill for primary, Outline for secondary.Cards: Subtle shadow (shadow-sm) growing to shadow-lg on hover.Icons: Lucide React (Stroke width 2px).
